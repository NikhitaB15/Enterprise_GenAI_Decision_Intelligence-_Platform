# Telco Retention & Pricing Policy 2024

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the retention strategies and pricing tiers for fiber optic and DSL customers. The goal is to reduce churn among high-value segments.

## 2. Pricing Tiers
### Fiber Optic
- **Standard Rate**: $90/month
- **Promo Rate**: $70/month for the first 12 months.
- **Bundle**: Internet + Phone + Streaming TV = $110/month.

### DSL
- **Standard Rate**: $45/month.
- **Loyalty Rate**: $35/month for customers with tenure > 2 years.

## 3. Retention Guidelines (Critical)
### Triggers for Intervention
Service agents should intervene if a customer meets ANY of the following criteria:
- **Tenure** is less than 6 months (High Risk Zone).
- **Monthly Charges** exceed $100.
- Customer has opened > 3 **Tech Support** tickets in the last 30 days.

### Approved Offers ("Save Tactics")
1. **The "Speed Upgrade"**: Offer a free upgrade to the next speed tier for 6 months.
2. **The "15% Discount"**: Authorized for customers with > 70 tenure months who threaten to cancel.
3. **Contract Buyout**: We will pay up to $200 in contract termination fees if switching from a competitor.

## 4. Tech Support Policy
- **Gold Tier**: Free 24/7 priority support for customers on 2-year contracts.
- **Silver Tier**: Standard support hours (8am - 8pm).
- **Charges**: On-site tech visits cost $99 unless the issue is verified as equipment failure.
