# Enterprise Retention Strategy: Tiered Intervention Model

## 1. Overview
This document outlines the standard operating procedures for customer retention. Our goal is to reduce churn by identifying "At-Risk" customers early and applying targeted interventions based on customer lifetime value (CLV) and risk score.

## 3. Intervention Policies
- **Discount Allocation**: Discounts must not exceed 25% of the annual contract value.
- **Support Scaling**: Customers with >5 open tickets in 30 days are automatically escalated to the Support Excellence Team regardless of churn score.
- **Competitor Matching**: We do not price-match competitors directly; focus must remain on unique value propositions (Security, Integration, Support).
